Company Overview

Where We Are Today

Least of These Carolinas is at a critical inflection point. After 12 years of operation and a year of significant change, we've experienced both tremendous growth and operational challenges.

This Has Been a Year of Major Change:

  • Purchased and moved into new headquarters building
  • Achieved 75% revenue growth to $1.2M
  • Expanded team to 15 employees (8.6 FTE)
  • Announced statewide expansion plan
  • Nominated for CFA True Inspiration Award
  • Established board committees
My Honest Assessment: We changed too much this year, too much, and it got to be chaotic. We operated in an emergency state almost all year. As we have moved into HQ, things are settling, but we need to stop, breathe, and make sure we are ready for expansion.
